FAQ

Does OfficeDigger change the original documents?
No! OfficeDigger always works on a copy of your documents. When you extract an embedded file, it stays in the document and a copy is saved.

Does OfficeDigger support AppleScript?
Not yet - it's on the roadmap for a future version.
